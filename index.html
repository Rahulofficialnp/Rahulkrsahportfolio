Temperature Based Automatic Fan Control

A Temperature Based Automatic Fan Control System is a system that automatically controls the speed of a fan based on the temperature of the surrounding environment. The system consists of a temperature sensor, a microcontroller, and a fan motor. The temperature sensor continuously measures the temperature of the surrounding environment and sends the data to the microcontroller. The microcontroller uses the data to determine the appropriate speed of the fan motor to maintain a desired temperature range.
